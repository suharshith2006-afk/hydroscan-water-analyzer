Who We Are

Intelligent Water Diagnostics

HydroScan is a pioneering utility engineered to bridge the gap between complex analytical lab procedures and real-time field deployments. By turning standard consumer smartphone cameras into highly calibrated colorimetric spectrometers, we put safety data directly into the hands of the public.

Our framework breaks down incoming video and photographic matrices into distinct RGB component layers, testing variance vectors against mathematical calibration curves. This enables fast, scalable analytical responses to protect communities globally.
